Why is the site shutting down? The longevity of the Burst series was not expected, and after these 3 years I feel (just) a little bit tired of Beyblade and maintaining the site, therefore I've decided to close it. It has nothing to do with legal issues or any 'bad' things. In addition, this is a 0-revenue but cost-bearing project...
當初沒想到 Burst 系列能如此長壽。過了數年，對陀螺及本網已有一點覺厭覺倦；加上這是一個零收入但要付錢（網域費、寄存費）的 project，故在此決定結束
